The Five Questions (Must Be Answered in Writing)
  1. What is the working diagnosis and your confidence level?
  2. What findings remain unexplained?
  3. What is the explicit plan to resolve them, with timeframe?
  4. If you refused a test/consult, what alternative pathway are you using?
  5. If de-escalating care, what objective evidence supports safety?
These questions cannot be dismissed. They become part of the permanent record. Saint Jude makes quiet death expensive.
